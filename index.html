An eSIM (embedded SIM) is a digital SIM that lets you activate a cellular plan without a physical SIM card. It's built directly into your device and can be activated instantly by scanning a QR code.

Most modern smartphones support eSIM, including iPhone XS and later, Google Pixel 3 and later, and Samsung Galaxy S20 and later. You can confirm eSIM support in your device settings under Mobile Data or Cellular.

Your plan starts when you first connect to a network in Japan, not when you purchase or install the eSIM. This means you can install it before your trip without losing any data days.

After purchase, your QR code is automatically generated via our eSIM provider and delivered to your email within minutes. Simply scan the QR code in your phone's Mobile Data settings to activate your eSIM.
