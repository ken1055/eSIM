For Apple Devices: Access [Settings] – [General] – [About] and scroll down. If you see "Network Provider Lock – No SIM restrictions", your phone supports eSIM.

For Samsung & Google Devices: Access [Settings] – [Connections] – [Mobile networks] – [Network operators]. If you see multiple available networks, your phone is likely unlocked and eSIM-compatible.

Complete your eSIM purchase in just three easy steps: Select the data plan → Process payment securely → Receive QR code via email within 15 minutes.

We will send you a confirmation email with a QR code after your purchase is completed. There are two ways to add an eSIM to your device. To activate the plan, ensure you are connected to a strong Wi-Fi signal and not in Airplane Mode.

For Apple: Go to [Settings] → [Cellular/Mobile Data] → [Add Cellular Plan] → [Scan QR Code] and scan the QR code received via email.

For Samsung: Go to [Settings] → [Connections] → [SIM Card Manager] → [Add a Mobile Plan] → [Scan Carrier QR code] and scan the QR code.
