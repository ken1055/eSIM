1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information when you use our services:

  • Contact information: Email address (used to deliver your QR code)
  • Payment information: Credit card details (encrypted and processed by Stripe — we do not store card data)
  • Device information: Device type, operating system, and device identifiers such as IMEI
  • Usage data: Data consumption and connection history
  • Access logs: IP address, browser type, and pages visited

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing, managing, and improving our eSIM service
  • Delivering QR codes and supporting eSIM activation
  • Providing customer support
  • Processing and confirming payments
  • Preventing fraud and ensuring security
  • Sending important service-related notices
  • Analyzing usage statistics to improve service quality

We will not use your personal information for any purpose beyond those listed above without your prior consent.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following cases:

  • With your consent

  • When required by law
  • When necessary to protect the life, safety, or property of individuals
Key service providers we work with:
  • Stripe, Inc. (payment processing)
  • Telecom carrier partners (eSIM network delivery)
  • Email delivery services (QR code distribution)

4. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your experience. Cookies are used for the following purposes:

You may disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features of the site may not function correctly as a result.

We store your personal information with appropriate security measures in place. We use SSL/TLS encrypted communications and implement technical and organizational safeguards to prevent unauthorized access, alteration, or data leaks.
